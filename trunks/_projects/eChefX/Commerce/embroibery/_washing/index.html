Make sure your washer and dryer are in good working order with no rough
areas that might snag fabric.
All linens should be free of excess food.
Shake to remove foreign debris.
Wash dark colors separately.
Wash like fibers together.
Do not mix Polyester with Poly/Cotton Blends, etc.
Do not overload washer or dryer.
Gradually cool linens in dryer to avoid temperature shock and excessive wrinkling.
Fold immediately after taking out of dryer.
See instructions below for specific fabrics.
Warm water wash (110º). Rinse on cool/permanent press cycle.
Use mild detergent only. Avoid high alkaline level.
No bleach, unless necessary for whites.
Tumble dry, low heat with cool down cycle.
Remove promptly from dryer. Do not over-dry.
Cool water wash (80-100º). Rinse on cool/permanent press cycle.
Use mild detergent only. Avoid high alkaline level.
No bleach, unless necessary for whites.
Tumble dry, low heat with cool down cycle.
Remove promptly from dryer. Do not over-dry.
